## 2024-03-20 - Accessible Chat Input
**Learning:** Adding ARIA labels to icon-only buttons significantly improves screen reader experience without visual clutter.
**Action:** Always check icon-only buttons for aria-labels.

## 2024-05-23 - Toggle Button State
**Learning:** Toggle buttons must use `aria-pressed` to communicate their state (on/off) to assistive technologies. Visual indicators alone are insufficient.
**Action:** When creating or modifying toggle buttons, ensure `aria-pressed` attribute reflects the active state.
