## 2024-03-20 - Accessible Chat Input
**Learning:** Adding ARIA labels to icon-only buttons significantly improves screen reader experience without visual clutter.
**Action:** Always check icon-only buttons for aria-labels.

## 2025-05-23 - Editor Toolbar Accessibility
**Learning:** Complex editor toolbars often contain many icon-only buttons (like view toggles, actions). These are critical for navigation but often overlooked.
**Action:** systematically audit header/toolbar components for missing accessible names.
